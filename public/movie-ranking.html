My top three Movies of all time

Scorpion

A sci-fi film that brings the genius in you when you follow the plot closely but that is if you are at least brilliant in the first place

Billions

If you are a market savvy person, this movie shows you the nitty gritty of could be or might not be going on in financial markets.

The Unthinkable

This movie is full of suspense about what extent the government can go through in other to deal with terrorist on the international level.
